About Us

Nimphie’s Oasis Wine & Liquor is a family-owned business with proud military roots. We hang our hats on excellent customer service and helping you bring seaside vibes to every occasion. Kick your feet up with us and escape reality.

The Story So Far

Nimphie’s Oasis was newly opened in 2024 and formerly known as "Thomason's Wine & Liquor" under the previous owner. While we are currently working on building our own brand, the store has a well-known, long-established presence with the local Eastmont community.
