About Us

Nimphie’s Oasis Wine & Liquor is a family-owned business with proud military roots. We hang our hats on excellent customer service and supplying you with seaside vibes for every occasion. Unwind, indulge, and let the spirits move you.

Our store was newly opened in 2024 and formerly known as "Thomason's Wine & Liquor" under the previous owner. While we are currently working on building our own brand, the store has a long-established presence in the Eastmont community.
